What We Do

We have been working with service instruments in the industry for the past ten years, providing reliable calibration services through our partnership with NABL-accredited tie-up labs. We offer onsite service and specialize in servicing all types of mechanical instruments using original spare parts to ensure accuracy and long-term performance.

Our Specialization

We specialize in vernier error correction and precise refixing of internal and external jaws, supported by a large stock of spare parts. Our highly experienced service personnel ensure accurate, reliable repairs, and we are well-known for delivering exceptional customer satisfaction.

Instrument Service

We provide expert servicing for vernier calipers, micrometers, plunger dials, lever dials, height gauges, and all types of precision measuring instruments to ensure accuracy and reliable performance.

Sales

We offer sales of leading brands such as Mitutoyo, Baker, Insize, Sylvac, MGW, and Yamayo, providing high-quality measuring instruments for diverse industrial needs.

Calibration

We provide calibration for all types of measuring instruments with reliable NABL-certified reports to ensure accuracy and compliance.

Error Correction

We offer precise error correction for vernier calipers, including ID, OD, and depth measurements, ensuring restored accuracy and reliable performance.
